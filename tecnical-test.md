AI AGENT BUILDER ASSIGNMENT


Context


You're joining a company that builds and sells practice management software for dental and medical clinics. Our customers are clinic owners, office managers, and front desk staff who handle scheduling, billing, patient records, and insurance on a daily basis. We want to use AI agents to transform how these clinics operate — and how we sell, support, and market to them.


This is not a research exercise. We want to see you build something real.


---


Your Task


Build a working AI agent (or system of agents) that addresses one of the following tracks. Pick the one that plays most to your strengths — or combine them if you're feeling ambitious.


Track A — Customer Support Agent

Build a conversational agent that handles common clinic inquiries: software questions, upgrading, billing FAQs, or escalation routing. The agent should handle multi-step conversations naturally and know when to hand off to a human.


Track B — Sales & Outreach Agent

Build an agent that helps a sales rep find and reach dental or medical clinics. It should research a prospect, score their fit, and draft a personalized outreach message. The output should feel human-written, not templated.


Track C — Marketing Agent

Build an agent that helps a small marketing team punch above its weight — whether that's identifying content opportunities for clinic managers, drafting a campaign, or nurturing leads based on their behavior.


---


Requirements


You can use Claude Code, Cursor, Windsurf, VSCode, or any IDE or tooling of your choice.


• Working agent: It must handle a real, multi-step workflow — not just answer a single question.

• Source code: Share a link to your repository (GitHub, GitLab, etc.).

• README: A short explanation of how to run your project.

• Write-up: A brief note (half a page to one page) covering what problem you chose, why, the key design decisions you made, and what you'd build or improve next.


Bonus (not required, but appreciated)

• Deploy it and share a live URL for us to test.

• Add a human-in-the-loop review step before any message is sent.

• Chain multiple agents together and show how they hand off to each other.

• Integrate with a real tool — Gmail, HubSpot, Slack, a calendar API, etc.

• Add an evaluation or feedback loop so the agent can improve over time.


---


What We're Evaluating


• Relevance: Does it reflect a real understanding of how dental or medical practices operate?

• Agent quality: Does it handle multi-step tasks, edge cases, and failures gracefully?

• Shipping instinct: Is it functional and usable, not just a polished demo?

• Creativity: Did you find an angle or workflow we hadn't thought of?


We're not looking for a perfect system. We're looking for someone who can identify a high-value workflow, build something real around it, and articulate a clear point of view on what to do next. If you made tradeoffs, tell us — that's part of the evaluation.
